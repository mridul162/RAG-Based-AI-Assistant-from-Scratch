## Key Nutrients

Pabnar Pure Gawa Ghee is a rich source of fat-soluble vitamins and short-to-medium-chain fatty acids due to its traditional preparation from indigenous Pabna cow milk.

* **Fat-Soluble Vitamins:** Contains significant amounts of **Vitamin A** (approx. 3,000 IU/100g), **Vitamin E**, and **Vitamin K2**, which support eye health, skin vitality, and calcium metabolism.
* **Butyric Acid:** Rich in butyrate, a short-chain fatty acid that aids gastrointestinal health, supports the gut microbiome, and reduces inflammation.
* **Conjugated Linoleic Acid (CLA):** Contains CLA, a polyunsaturated fatty acid known for its antioxidant properties and role in metabolic health.
* **Glycemic Index (GI):** **0 (Zero)**. Ghee contains no carbohydrates or sugars, resulting in a zero glycemic impact, making it safe for diabetic diets.

> **Batch and Seasonal Variation Note:** > Because this ghee is processed using traditional slow-cooking Bilona methods from grass-fed indigenous cows, minor variations in color, granular texture, fatty acid profile, and vitamin concentration may occur. Ghee produced during the monsoon season (when fresh green forage is abundant) typically exhibits a deeper golden hue and higher vitamin A content.
