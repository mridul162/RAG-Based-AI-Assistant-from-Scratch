# Shipping
## Temperature requirements

- **Ideal shipping temperature**: Maintain between 10–30°C (50–86°F) during transit. Ghee is stable but can melt or separate if exposed to high temperatures (>35°C / 95°F) for extended periods.
- **Hot weather protection**: Use insulated packaging or ship during cooler parts of the day in summer months. Avoid leaving packages in direct sunlight or unventilated vehicles.
- No refrigeration or cold-chain shipping required under normal conditions.

## Packaging notes

- Products are shipped in their original sealed glass or metal containers.
- Outer packaging includes sturdy cardboard boxes with sufficient cushioning (bubble wrap, foam inserts, or shredded paper) to prevent breakage and leakage.
- All jars are individually wrapped and secured to minimize movement during transit.
- Fragility: Glass jars are moderately fragile—handle with care to avoid cracks or impacts.

## Regulatory considerations

- **Domestic (Bangladesh)**: Complies with BSTI and local food safety standards. No special permits required for standard orders.
- **Food safety**: Pure deshi ghee is considered a low-risk, shelf-stable product.
- **Labeling**: All packages include product details, ingredients (100% pure cow milk ghee), and handling instructions.
- Seasonal restrictions: None. Product is available year-round.

## International shipping

- Available to select countries. Contact support for availability and quotes.
- **Customs & import**: Dairy products may require import permits or face restrictions in certain countries (e.g., EU, USA, Middle East). Customers are responsible for complying with destination country regulations, including any duties or quarantine rules for animal-derived products.
- **Documentation**: Commercial invoice and certificate of origin provided upon request.
- **Transit time**: Typically 5–15 business days depending on destination and shipping method. Delays possible due to customs clearance.
- **Liability**: We recommend insurance for international shipments. Claims for damage must be reported within 48 hours of receipt with photos.

**Note**: Shipping conditions subject to carrier availability and weather. For best quality, order during milder seasons when possible.
