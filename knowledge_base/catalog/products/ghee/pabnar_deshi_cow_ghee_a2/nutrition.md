## Key Nutrients

### 1. Pure A2 Beta-Casein Source

While the final ghee contains zero protein, it is crafted exclusively from the milk of indigenous Pabna cows containing the **A2 beta-casein protein**. The traditional clarifying process removes all milk solids, making it highly bio-compatible for individuals who are sensitive to conventional A1 dairy products.

### 2. High Smoke Point

Pabnar Deshi Cow Ghee has a high smoke point of **252°C (485°F)**. This ensures that the fatty acids remain stable under high-heat cooking, preventing the formation of harmful free radicals.

### 3. Short and Medium-Chain Fatty Acids

* **Butyric Acid:** A short-chain fatty acid that supports gut health, aids digestion, and possesses natural anti-inflammatory properties.
* **Lauric and Caprylic Acids:** Provide a readily available, slow-burning energy source for the body.

### 4. Fat-Soluble Vitamins

Rich in natural **Vitamins A, D, and E**, which are essential for immune function, skin health, and cellular repair.

### 5. Glycemic Index (GI)

* **Glycemic Index:** 0 (Zero)
* **Glycemic Load:** 0
Ghee does not trigger an insulin spike, making it fully compatible with ketogenic, low-carb, and diabetic dietary regimens.
