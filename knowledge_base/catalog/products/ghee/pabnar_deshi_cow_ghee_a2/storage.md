# Storage
## Ideal conditions

Store Pabnar Deshi Cow Ghee A2 in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat sources. 

- **Temperature:** Below 25–30°C (77–86°F) is ideal. The ghee will naturally solidify in cooler temperatures and soften/melt in warmer conditions — this is normal for pure traditional ghee and does not affect quality.
- **Humidity:** Low humidity environment to prevent moisture absorption.
- **Light:** Keep away from direct sunlight to preserve color, aroma, and nutritional properties.
- **Position:** Upright in a stable location. No refrigeration required for normal use, though refrigeration is acceptable in extremely hot climates (above 35°C consistently).

## Container recommendations

- **Original packaging:** Keep in the supplied food-grade glass jar or metal tin with tight-fitting lid for best results.
- **Transfer:** If transferring, use clean, dry, airtight glass or stainless steel containers. Avoid plastic containers for long-term storage as they may affect flavor.
- **After opening:** Always reseal tightly after each use to minimize air exposure. Use a clean, dry spoon to avoid introducing moisture.

## Shelf life

- **Unopened:** 12–18 months from manufacturing date when stored properly.
- **Opened:** Best used within 3–6 months for optimal aroma, flavor, and granular texture. Can remain safe for up to 9–12 months with proper care.
- **Note:** Shelf life is influenced by storage conditions. Premium traditional ghee like this maintains quality longer than blended or lower-purity varieties due to its low moisture content.

## Signs of spoilage

- **Smell:** Rancid, sour, or off odor (fresh ghee has a pleasant nutty aroma).
- **Appearance:** Unusual discoloration (beyond normal golden/yellow), visible mold, or excessive separation that does not resolve upon gentle warming.
- **Taste:** Bitter or soapy flavor.
- **Texture:** If it develops unusual graininess or sliminess (rare).

Discard if any spoilage signs appear. Pure deshi ghee is highly stable, so spoilage is uncommon when stored correctly.
