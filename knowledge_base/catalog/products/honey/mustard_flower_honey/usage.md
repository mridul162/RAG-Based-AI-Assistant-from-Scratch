# Usage
## Best uses

<section>

### Direct consumption

* Enjoy 1–2 teaspoons directly for a simple and convenient way to consume honey.
* Suitable as part of a daily routine or whenever a natural sweet taste is desired.

### In warm beverages

* Mix into warm water, lemon water, herbal tea, or milk.
* The strong floral aroma of Mustard Flower Honey pairs particularly well with winter beverages.

### As a natural sweetener

* Use as an alternative to refined sugar in tea, coffee, smoothies, and homemade drinks.
* Adds both sweetness and a distinctive floral flavor.

### Breakfast companion

* Drizzle over bread, toast, pancakes, waffles, porridge, oats, or cereals.
* Combines well with yogurt, fruits, and nuts.

### Cooking and baking

* Can be used in cakes, cookies, muffins, and homemade desserts.
* Suitable for marinades, glazes, salad dressings, and sauces.

### Traditional household use

* Commonly mixed with warm water, lemon, or ginger-based drinks during colder months.
* Frequently included in traditional winter food preparations.

</section>

## Recipe ideas

<section>

### Honey Lemon Drink

**Ingredients**

* 1 glass warm water
* 1–2 teaspoons Mustard Flower Honey
* Juice of half a lemon

**Method**

1. Add honey and lemon juice to warm water.
2. Stir until mixed.
3. Serve immediately.

---

### Honey Ginger Beverage

**Ingredients**

* 1 cup warm water
* 1 teaspoon grated ginger
* 1–2 teaspoons Mustard Flower Honey

**Method**

1. Infuse ginger in warm water for several minutes.
2. Strain if desired.
3. Add honey and stir before serving.

---

### Yogurt, Fruit & Honey Bowl

**Ingredients**

* Plain yogurt
* Seasonal fruits
* 1 tablespoon Mustard Flower Honey
* Optional nuts or seeds

**Method**

1. Combine yogurt and fruits in a bowl.
2. Drizzle honey on top.
3. Add nuts or seeds if desired.

---

### Honey Mustard Dressing

**Ingredients**

* 1 tablespoon Mustard Flower Honey
* 1 tablespoon mustard
* 2 tablespoons olive oil
* A small amount of lemon juice

**Method**

1. Whisk all ingredients together.
2. Use as a dressing for salads or roasted vegetables.

---

### Honey Toast

**Ingredients**

* Bread or toast
* 1–2 teaspoons Mustard Flower Honey

**Method**

1. Spread honey over freshly toasted bread.
2. Serve as a quick breakfast or snack.

</section>

## What to avoid

<section>

### Avoid adding to very hot liquids

* Excessive heat may reduce some of the naturally occurring enzymes and aroma compounds found in honey.
* Allow boiling beverages to cool slightly before adding honey.

### Do not feed to infants under 12 months

* Honey is not recommended for infants younger than one year of age.

### Avoid contamination

* Always use a clean, dry spoon when handling honey.
* Introducing moisture can affect quality over time.

### Do not refrigerate unnecessarily

* Store in a cool, dry place at room temperature.
* Refrigeration may accelerate crystallization.

### Do not mistake crystallization for spoilage

* Natural crystallization is common in pure Mustard Flower Honey, especially during cooler weather.
* If desired, place the sealed container in mildly warm water to soften crystallized honey.

### Avoid prolonged exposure to sunlight

* Store away from direct sunlight and excessive heat to help preserve flavor, aroma, and quality.

</section>

## Storage tips

<section>

* Keep the container tightly sealed after use.
* Store in a cool, dry, and clean environment.
* Protect from moisture, direct sunlight, and excessive heat.
* When stored properly, honey can maintain its quality for an extended period.

</section>
