## Price Drivers

The pricing of Mustard Flower Honey is influenced by several distinct agricultural, seasonal, and operational factors:

* **Seasonal Yield Windows:** Mustard flower honey is a strictly winter specialty honey. Harvesting occurs within a narrow 6 to 8-week timeframe (typically between late November and January) when mustard fields are in full bloom across regions like North Bengal, Rajshahi, and Dinajpur. Variations in winter weather directly impact yield and market price.
* **Extraction and Processing Formats:** The honey is minimally processed to maintain its natural raw properties, retaining beneficial enzymes and pollen. Cold extraction processes require meticulous labor to prevent overheating, preserving the natural quality but increasing production times relative to mass-processed commercial brands.
* **High Liquidity and Supply Volume:** Because mustard cultivation spans vast agricultural areas in Bangladesh, the seasonal supply volume is relatively high. This abundant localized supply positions mustard honey as one of the most cost-effective and affordable pure honeys on the market.
* **Storage and Crystallization Management:** Mustard honey features a naturally high glucose-to-fructose ratio, causing it to crystallize rapidly into a thick, creamy paste during colder months. Storing and handling a highly crystalline product requires specific temperature-controlled environments to keep it stable before delivery.

## Value Justification

While mass-market adulterated syrups or ultra-filtered commercial honeys may retail at lower price points, our Mustard Flower Honey provides measurable, uncompromised value:

* **100% Purity and Traceability:** Gathered directly from the blooming mustard fields of Bangladesh. We ensure zero sugar-syrup feeding, zero artificial blending, and no high-heat pasteurization that kills beneficial nutrients.
* **Intact Health Properties:** High concentrations of natural antioxidants, active enzymes (diastase, invertase), and trace minerals make it highly effective for traditional winter remedies, immunity boosting, and cough relief.
* **Rapid Crystallization Proof:** The immediate crystallization of this honey serves as a visual guarantee of purity. Adulterated or heavily processed honeys remain artificially liquid; our honey’s fast transition to a creamy state verifies its authentic glucose-heavy floral origin.
