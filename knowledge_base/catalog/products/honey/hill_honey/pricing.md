# Pricing
## Price Drivers | মূল্য নির্ধারণের নিয়ামকসমূহ

The pricing of our Premium Wild Hill Honey (খাঁটি পাহাড়ি মধু) is fundamentally driven by its unique sourcing method, ecological limitations, and demanding logistics:

* **Manual Sourcing & Danger Premiums:** Unlike farmed honey, this honey is harvested from the wild by local tribal communities in the deep, remote forests of Bandarban, Rangamati, and Sylhet. Harvesters scale high mountain cliffs and immense forest canopies to manually collect nests from wild giant honeybees (*Apis dorsata laboriosa*), requiring specialized traditional skills and carrying high physical risks.
* **Seasonal Scarcity:** Wild hill honey is strictly seasonal. Harvests are entirely dependent on natural blooming cycles of mountain flora and weather conditions in the Chittagong Hill Tracts. Production volumes fluctuate annually and cannot be scaled up artificially.
* **Purity & Lab Testing Costs:** Every batch undergoes strict quality control and laboratory testing to verify a stable moisture content (below 20%) and ensure total freedom from synthetic sugar feeding, adulteration, or chemical residues.
* **Complex Logistics:** Transporting fragile glass containers from remote, unpaved hilly terrains to urban distribution hubs requires multi-layered protective packaging and careful transit handling, which increases overhead costs compared to flatland agricultural products.

## Value Justification | মূল্যের যৌক্তিকতা

While commercial alternatives exist at lower price points, the investment in Wild Hill Honey translates directly into verifiable product integrity:

* **100% Unadulterated Organic State:** Commercial bee farms often feed bees sugar syrup during off-seasons to maintain continuous production. Our Hill Honey is entirely forged from wild jungle nectar, meaning it contains zero trace antibiotics, agricultural pesticides, or added industrial sugars.
* **High Antioxidant Density:** The diverse multi-floral diet of jungle bees infuses this dark amber honey with significantly higher concentrations of medicinal polyphenols, active enzymes, and bioactive trace minerals than single-crop honey variants.
* **Fair Trade & Tribal Community Support:** A major portion of the premium price directly compensates the indigenous harvesting families in the hilly tracts, ensuring sustainable foraging practices and supporting local marginalized economies.
