# Usage
## Best Uses | ব্যবহারের সেরা উপায়

<section>

Hill Honey (পাহাড়ি মধু) is a versatile natural sweetener that can be enjoyed directly or incorporated into everyday foods and beverages. Its rich flavor and strong floral-herbal aroma make it suitable for both traditional and modern uses.

### Direct Consumption

* Consume straight from the spoon for a quick source of natural sweetness.
* Commonly enjoyed in the morning or between meals.

### In Warm Beverages

* Mix into warm water for a simple honey drink.
* Add to herbal tea, black tea, green tea, or lemon water.
* Stir in after the beverage has cooled slightly to help preserve honey's natural characteristics.

### With Breakfast Foods

* Drizzle over toast, flatbread, pancakes, waffles, or porridge.
* Mix into yogurt, oats, granola, or fruit bowls.
* Use as a topping for smoothies and smoothie bowls.

### In Cooking and Baking

* Use as a natural sweetener in homemade desserts.
* Add to salad dressings, marinades, and sauces.
* Incorporate into energy bites, protein snacks, or healthy homemade treats.

### Traditional Pairings

* Honey with warm water and lemon.
* Honey with black seed (kalonji).
* Honey mixed with nuts and dried fruits.
* Honey with ginger in warm drinks.

</section>

## Dosage / Serving Suggestions | পরিবেশন ও গ্রহণের পরামর্শ

<section>

The appropriate serving amount depends on individual dietary needs and preferences.

| Usage Type          | Suggested Serving                     |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Direct consumption  | 1–2 teaspoons (5–10 g)                |
| Honey water         | 1 tablespoon in a glass of warm water |
| Tea or herbal drink | 1–2 teaspoons per cup                 |
| Yogurt or oats      | 1–2 tablespoons                       |
| Smoothies           | 1 tablespoon                          |
| Breakfast topping   | 1–2 tablespoons                       |

### Daily Use Tips

* Enjoy in moderation as part of a balanced diet.
* Adjust serving size based on sweetness preference.
* Remember that honey is a source of natural sugars and calories.

</section>

## Recipe Ideas | রেসিপি আইডিয়া

<section>

### 1. Hill Honey Lemon Drink

**Ingredients**

* 1 tablespoon Hill Honey
* Juice of ½ lemon
* 1 glass warm water

**Method**

1. Add lemon juice to warm water.
2. Stir in honey.
3. Mix well and serve.

### 2. Honey Nut Breakfast Bowl

**Ingredients**

* Yogurt
* Mixed nuts
* Seasonal fruits
* 1–2 tablespoons Hill Honey

**Method**

1. Place yogurt in a bowl.
2. Add fruits and nuts.
3. Drizzle honey on top before serving.

### 3. Honey Ginger Tea

**Ingredients**

* Fresh ginger slices
* Hot water
* 1 teaspoon Hill Honey

**Method**

1. Steep ginger in hot water.
2. Allow the tea to cool slightly.
3. Stir in honey and enjoy.

### 4. Honey Salad Dressing

**Ingredients**

* 1 tablespoon Hill Honey
* 2 tablespoons olive oil
* 1 tablespoon lemon juice
* Pinch of salt

**Method**

1. Whisk all ingredients together.
2. Drizzle over fresh salads.

### 5. Homemade Honey Nuts

**Ingredients**

* Mixed nuts (almonds, cashews, walnuts, peanuts)
* Hill Honey

**Method**

1. Mix nuts with honey.
2. Serve as a snack or breakfast topping.

</section>

## What to Avoid | যা এড়িয়ে চলবেন

<section>

### Avoid Excessive Heat

* Do not boil honey directly.
* Avoid adding honey to extremely hot liquids immediately after boiling.
* Excessive heat may affect flavor, aroma, and naturally occurring compounds.

### Avoid Moisture Contamination

* Always use a clean, dry spoon.
* Do not allow water to enter the container, as excess moisture may affect quality.

### Avoid Improper Storage

* Store in a tightly sealed container.
* Keep in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
* Refrigeration is generally not necessary.

### Do Not Worry About Crystallization

* Natural crystallization is normal in pure honey.
* Place the jar in lukewarm water if a smoother texture is desired.

### Important Safety Note

* Not suitable for infants under 12 months of age.
* Individuals with known honey or bee-product allergies should consult a healthcare professional before consumption.

</section>
