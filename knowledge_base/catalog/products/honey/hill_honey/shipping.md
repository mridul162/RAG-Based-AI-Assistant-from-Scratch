# Shipping
## Temperature Requirements | তাপমাত্রা সংক্রান্ত সতর্কতা

Raw, unpasteurized wild hill honey is highly sensitive to environmental extremes during transit:

* **Heat Exposure:** Shipping vehicles or storage hubs where temperatures exceed **40°C** must be avoided. Prolonged high heat degrades the therapeutic enzymes and can compromise the flavor profile.
* **Climate-Controlled Handling:** During the peak summer months in Bangladesh (April to September), packages should be prioritized for fast transit or stored in cool shaded zones during sorting.
* **Transit Pressure:** Since raw honey contains active micro-compounds, excessive heat combined with vibration can cause slight gas expansion inside the jar. Shipments must not be left inside unventilated delivery vans for extended periods.

## Packaging Notes | প্যাকেজিং ও ভঙ্গুরতা বিষয়ক নির্দেশনা

Because this product is distributed primarily in glass jars to protect its chemical integrity, strict packaging safeguards apply:

* **Fragility Level:** High. Glass containers require multi-layered cushion wraps.
* **Primary Seal:** Every jar features a pressure-sensitive induction inner seal beneath the cap to block moisture absorption and prevent leakage under transit pressure shifts.
* **Secondary Buffering:** Jars are wrapped in double-layered bubble wrap or corrugated paper sleeves before being placed inside custom corrugated cardboard shipping boxes.
* **Orientation Markings:** Shipping boxes must be explicitly stamped with **"Fragile"** and **"This Side Up"** directional arrows to minimize upside-down storage during sorting.

## Regulatory Considerations | নিয়ন্ত্রণ ও আইনি বিধিমালা

Domestic distribution within Bangladesh must adhere to local agricultural and wildlife sourcing frameworks:

* **Sourcing Compliance:** Our wild hill honey is ethically sourced from local tribal communities operating under traditional foraging rights in the forest reserves of Bandarban, Rangamati, and Sylhet.
* **Certification Requirements:** For bulk domestic commercial transport, shipments carry verified lab clearance certificates proving the moisture content falls within the stable range (under 20%) and that no synthetic sugar syrups are adulterated.
* **Inter-District Transit:** Standard consumer shipments do not require special regional permits for courier transport between hilly regions and metropolitan areas like Dhaka or Chittagong.

## International Shipping | আন্তর্জাতিক শিপিং নীতিমাল

Shipping pure, raw honey outside of Bangladesh is subject to stringent global biosecurity laws for animal and food products:

| Destination Region | Regulatory Requirement | Feasibility |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **European Union (EU)** | Requires strict third-country listing verification for honey imports, heavy-metal residue testing, and veterinary health certificates. | Restructured/Bulk only; restricted for individual courier packages. |
| **United States (US)** | Requires US FDA Prior Notice filing for every individual food shipment entering the country. | Feasible via authorized international couriers (DHL/FedEx) with clearance documentation. |
| **Middle East / Gulf** | Compliance with GSO food standards, customs declaration as "Raw Natural Bee Honey", and clear country of origin labeling. | Highly feasible; standard clearance protocols apply. |

> **Important Notice for International Buyers:** Liquid food items, specifically unpasteurized honey, are categorized as restricted biological products in several countries (e.g., Australia, New Zealand) to protect local bee populations from pathogens. Customers are strictly responsible for verifying local customs import allowances before placing international delivery requests.
