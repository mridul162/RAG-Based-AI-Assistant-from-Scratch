# Aliases
## Common Names | সাধারণ নামসমূহ

These are the primary standard names used globally and locally across retail, labeling, and customer service channels for this product.

* **Hill Honey:** The primary English trade name, indicating its origin from elevated terrains and hills.
* **Pahari Modhu (পাহাড়ি মধু):** The standard Bengali name and its direct English transliteration, widely recognized across Bangladesh.
* **Jungle Honey (জংলি মধু):** A common descriptive name reflecting that the honey is sourced from untamed, deep forest canopies rather than commercial agricultural farms.
* **Wild Honey:** A general category name used by customers seeking non-cultivated, raw honey varieties.

---

## Regional Names | আঞ্চলিক ও ভাষাগত নাম

Names categorized by language, dialect, and the specific geographic source regions within Bangladesh where the honey is harvested.

### Language Variants

* **Bengali (বাংলা):** পাহাড়ি মধু, জংলি মধু, পাহাড়ের মধু, খাঁটি পাহাড়ি মধু (Pure Hill Honey).
* **Banglish (Transliterated):** Pahari modhu, pahari modhu, jongli modhu, paharer modhu.

### Region-Specific Associations

* **Chittagong Hill Tracts Honey:** Refers specifically to harvests coming out of the southeastern hilly landscapes of Bangladesh.
* **Bandarban Wild Honey:** Sourced from the deep forests and high altitudes of the Bandarban district.
* **Rangamati Hill Honey:** Sourced from the forest ranges surrounding the Rangamati hill tracts.
* **Sylhet Forest Honey:** Sourced from the tropical forest pockets and hillocks within the greater Sylhet region.

---

## Scientific & Trade Names | বৈজ্ঞানিক ও বাণিজ্যিক নাম

Technical classifications used for inventory tracking, regulatory compliance, and biological identification within the RAG knowledge base.

* **Apis dorsata Laboriosa Honey:** The scientific designation corresponding to the wild giant Asian honeybees that construct open-air nests on high mountain cliffs and tall forest trees in the region.
* **Multiforall Wild Forest Honey:** The technical trade definition indicating the honey contains nectar from mixed, uncultivated jungle flora and wild medicinal herbs rather than a single crop source.
* **Raw Unpasteurized Hilly Honey:** The commercial grade indicating that the product has not undergone industrial heat processing or micro-filtration.
