## Temperature Requirements

Because this is raw, unprocessed honey, keeping it at the right temperature during transit ensures it retains its natural aroma, texture, and wholesome properties.

* **Optimal Storage Temperature:** The honey should be transported and stored between **15°C to 25°C**.
* **Heat Sensitivity:** Please protect the package from direct sunlight and prolonged exposure to high heat (above 40°C), as extreme heat can darken the honey and alter its natural enzymes. 
* **Cold Behavior:** In colder weather, raw honey may naturally start to crystallize. This is entirely normal and a proof of its purity. If this happens, simply place the honey jar in a bowl of warm water.
* **Seasonal Restrictions:** There are **no seasonal shipping restrictions** for this item; it is safely available for delivery all year round.

## International Shipping

Hasanah Mart primarily focuses on providing fast, dependable home delivery to families within our local service areas (including Narayanganj and surrounding regions). If you are looking to ship this product internationally, please keep the following rules in mind:

* **Liquid & Food Restrictions:** Many international couriers and customs authorities classify honey under strict biosecurity or "liquid food" regulations. 
* **Customs Documentation:** International shipments generally require an official certificate of origin, food safety clearance, and a detailed commercial invoice.
* **Receiver Responsibility:** Customers are advised to check the import regulations of the destination country before arranging international freight, as customs clearance rules for honey vary widely across regions.
