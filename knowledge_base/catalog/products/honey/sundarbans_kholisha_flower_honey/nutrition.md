Sundarbans Kholisha Flower Honey is more than just a natural sweetener. It contains a variety of trace elements that offer wholesome goodness for the entire family:

* **Natural Enzymes:** Active enzymes like diastase and invertase are preserved in this raw honey, aiding in easy digestion.

### Important Family Safety Note
* **Infant Safety:** Raw honey should never be fed to infants under 12 months of age, as their digestive systems are not yet mature enough to process raw honey safely.
