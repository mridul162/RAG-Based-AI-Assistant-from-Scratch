## When to Choose Kholisha Honey

Kholisha honey from Hasanah Mart is a good fit for your family if:

**You want to know where your food comes from.** This honey has a clear, specific origin — a named forest, a documented harvesting tradition, a traceable supply chain. That is not something most honey on the market can offer.

**You prefer food that is minimally processed.** Raw, unheated, cloth-strained — it reaches you as close to its natural state as practical. If your family avoids heavily processed foods, this is consistent with that.

**You want a mild, everyday honey.** Kholisha has a gentle flavour that works well for daily use — in tea, with food, or on its own. It is not sharp or overpowering, which makes it easy for the whole family including children over 1 year old.

**You want genuine Bangladeshi origin without paying an import premium.** You get wild-harvested, single-source, raw honey — the same qualities that make imported premium honey expensive — at a fraction of the price, and produced right here at home.

**You shop with Hasanah Mart for convenience.** We deliver it fresh to your door. No hunting around for a trustworthy seller, no uncertainty about what you are getting.

## When Another Option May Be Better

We would rather be honest with you than oversell.

**Choose local farmed honey if budget is the primary concern.** If your family needs honey purely as a sweetener and price is the deciding factor, farmed honey at ৳300–৳600/kg does the job. Just buy from a brand with BSTI certification and be aware that adulteration is common at the lower end of the market.

**Choose Keora or Goran honey if you prefer a stronger, bolder flavour.** Some families — particularly those who use honey in cooking or prefer a more intense taste — find Kholisha too mild. Keora honey from the Sundarbans has a more robust, distinctive character. The sourcing challenges are similar, so apply the same scrutiny when buying.

**Choose certified Manuka or Sidr honey if you need internationally verified quality for a specific purpose.** These honeys carry internationally recognised grading systems (MGO/UMF for Manuka) that are independently lab-tested and legally protected. If you are looking for a honey with a certified and verified potency level — for example, for someone in your family with a specific health-related reason advised by a doctor — certified imported honey offers verification that domestic honey currently cannot match. The cost is significantly higher, and you will need to import or buy through a verified retailer.

| Bold, strong flavour | Keora or Goran Sundarbans honey |
