## Health benefits

Kholisha flower honey can be used as a natural sweetener in tea, milk, desserts, and homemade drinks instead of refined sugar.

Honey naturally contains carbohydrates and sugars that can provide quick everyday energy when consumed as part of regular meals.

Collected from flower nectar in the Sundarbans region, this honey is valued for its natural origin and traditional use in Bangladeshi households.

### Comforting in warm drinks
Many families enjoy honey mixed with warm water, tea, or lemon drinks during seasonal weather changes.

### Easy to include in daily meals
Can be enjoyed in moderate amounts with breakfast foods, snacks, and homemade recipes.
