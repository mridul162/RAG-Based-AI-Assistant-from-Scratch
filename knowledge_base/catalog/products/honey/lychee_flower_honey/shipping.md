# Shipping
# Lychee Flower Honey (লিচু ফুলের মধু) - Shipping and Logistics Manual

This document defines the logistical rules, protective packaging standards, and regulatory considerations for shipping Lychee Flower Honey within Bangladesh and internationally. Honey requires explicit handling protocols to mitigate thermal risk and fragile container breakage.

---

## Temperature Requirements

While honey is highly shelf-stable, its physical properties alter under extreme environmental changes during transit.

* **Transit Thermal Range:** Maintain shipment environments below **35°C** wherever possible. Extended exposure to high heat within closed delivery vans or cargo holds accelerates the formation of Hydroxymethylfurfural (HMF), causing the honey to lose its delicate fruity-floral aroma and turn dark brown.
* **Avoid Freezing Air Cargo:** Unpressurized or unheated aircraft cargo areas can lower temperatures below **10°C**. While this does not damage the honey chemically, it accelerates natural crystallization, converting the liquid honey into a semi-solid opaque state that may surprise customers upon unboxing.
* **Seasonal Delays:** Because this product is highly seasonal, transit volumes peak during the warm summer months in Bangladesh (April through June). Logistics partners must prioritize well-ventilated, shaded dispatch queues over open-air storage zones.

## Packaging Notes

To handle transit shocks and prevent stickiness from leaks, follow these structural packaging rules for all variants (250g, 500g, 1kg):

### 1. Primary Seal Verification

* Every jar must feature an induction heat-sealed inner foil cover beneath the cap to eliminate leaking under pressure shifts.
* Apply a tamper-evident shrink sleeve or localized security tape over the lid closure to block unthreading during courier friction.

### 2. Shock and Drop Protection

* **Glass Jars:** Wrap each glass variant in a minimum of **3 layers of 10mm bubble wrap**, secured with heavy packaging tape. Place corrugated dividers inside bulk boxes to stop glass-on-glass impacts.
* **Plastic Jars:** Wrap in 2 layers of bubble wrap. Plastic variants absorb drops better but are prone to cracking under heavy vertical crush loads.

### 3. Outer Box Specifications

* Pack the wrapped units into double-walled corrugated cardboard shipper boxes (minimum 150 BCT rating).
* Fill all internal voids with air pillows or crumpled kraft paper to halt movement within the box.
* Mark the outer carton with prominent handling labels: **"FRAGILE — GLASS"**, **"LIQUID — DO NOT DROP"**, and orientation arrows **"THIS SIDE UP ↑"**.

## Regulatory Considerations

Shipping food items within Bangladesh requires adherence to domestic commercial transport laws.

* **BSTI Compliance:** All shipments must originate from batches that conform to Bangladesh Standards and Testing Institution (BSTI) honey regulations regarding moisture levels (under 20% to prevent active fermentation inside sealed containers).
* **Documentation:** Commercial freight manifests must explicitly list the product name in both English and Bengali (*Lychee Flower Honey / লিচু ফুলের মধু*) alongside its designated H.S. Code to ensure seamless inspection by highway authorities or transport security.

---

## International Shipping

Exporting honey outside Bangladesh is subject to strict biosecurity, sanitary, and phytosanitary controls depending on the destination country.
