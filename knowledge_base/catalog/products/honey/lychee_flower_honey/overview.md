# Overview
## Overview

<section>

Lychee Flower Honey (লিচু ফুলের মধু) is a seasonal monofloral honey collected by honey bees primarily from the nectar of lychee (litchi) blossoms during the short flowering period of lychee orchards in Bangladesh. It is one of the most popular floral honeys in the country, valued for its light color, delicate sweetness, and naturally fruity-floral aroma.

This honey is typically harvested during the lychee blooming season, when large numbers of bees forage on lychee flowers. Because the flowering window is relatively short, Lychee Flower Honey is considered a seasonal product and is available only in limited quantities each year.

Compared to many darker honey varieties, Lychee Flower Honey usually has a lighter appearance and a milder flavor profile. Its pleasant taste and smooth texture make it suitable for everyday consumption, whether taken directly, mixed into beverages, or used as a natural sweetener.

</section>

<section>

One of the defining features of Lychee Flower Honey is its distinctive floral aroma. The fragrance is generally lighter and more delicate than that of many wildflower or forest honeys. The flavor is sweet without being overpowering, making it appealing to a wide range of consumers.

As a natural honey, variations in color, aroma, and density may occur from season to season depending on weather conditions, flowering intensity, and regional differences in nectar sources. Such variations are normal and do not indicate quality issues.

</section>

## Origin & background

<section>

Bangladesh is well known for its lychee cultivation, particularly in regions with extensive seasonal orchards. During the flowering season, beekeepers place bee colonies near these orchards so that bees can collect nectar directly from blooming lychee flowers.

The resulting honey reflects the characteristics of the floral source and the local environment. Since lychee blossoms are available only for a limited period each year, production is naturally restricted to the harvest season. This seasonal nature contributes to the uniqueness and demand for Lychee Flower Honey.

Although classified as a monofloral honey, trace amounts of nectar from nearby flowering plants may occasionally be present, which is common in naturally produced honey.

</section>

## Why it matters

<section>

Lychee Flower Honey stands out because of its seasonal origin, light appearance, smooth texture, and pleasant floral-fruity aroma. It represents a distinctive floral honey variety produced from one of Bangladesh's most important fruit crops.

For consumers seeking a mild, approachable honey with a naturally delicate flavor profile, Lychee Flower Honey offers a recognizable and consistent choice. Its connection to the annual lychee flowering season also makes it a notable example of Bangladesh's floral honey diversity and traditional beekeeping practices.

</section>
