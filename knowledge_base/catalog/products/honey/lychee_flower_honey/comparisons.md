## When to Choose This Product

<section id="when-to-choose">

Choose **Lychee Flower Honey** when:

- You prefer a **mild, light-flavoured honey** that does not overpower food or drinks. Its delicate floral-fruity profile makes it well-suited for use in tea, milk, smoothies, and dressings where a strong honey taste is unwanted.
- You want **a distinctly seasonal, single-origin honey** with a unique character tied to a specific floral source and harvest period.
- You are buying for **children or individuals sensitive to strong flavours** — its gentle taste is generally more palatable than forest or black seed honeys.
- **Price is a factor** without wanting to compromise on a named floral honey — it is typically more affordable than Sundarban or black seed varieties while still being a premium single-source product.
- You are looking for a **recognisable, locally popular gifting option** — লিচু ফুলের মধু has strong cultural recognition in Bangladesh.
