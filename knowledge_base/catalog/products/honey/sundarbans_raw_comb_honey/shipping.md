# Shipping
## Temperature Requirements

Because Sundarbans Raw Comb Honey is completely unpasteurized, unheated, and biological, it retains active enzymes and natural gasses. Transit conditions must minimize temperature spikes to protect product integrity.

* **Avoid Extreme Heat:** Exposure to ambient temperatures above **35°C** during transit can soften the natural wax structures of the honeycomb, causing them to collapse and dissolve fully into the liquid honey.
* **Expansion Risk:** High heat can trigger natural gas release from active yeasts within raw honey. This causes the liquid to expand, which may push against seals or induce minor leakages if transit vehicles lack basic climate control or ventilation.
* **Seasonal Dispatch Restrictions:** During peak summer months in Bangladesh (April to August), long-distance ground shipments are scheduled for early morning or night dispatches whenever possible to prevent packages from sitting in non-air-conditioned warehouses or delivery vans during midday heat spikes.

## Packaging Notes

To mitigate the fragility of glass containers and prevent sticky spills, specific structural packing layers are mandatory for all domestic shipments.

* **Leak-Proof Induction Seals:** Every jar features a tight inner foil or plastic induction seal underneath the lid to prevent leakage caused by pressure shifts or vibrations during road transit.
* **Dual-Layer Cushioning:**
* **Primary Layer:** Jars are wrapped in a minimum of two layers of heavy-duty bubble wrap, taped securely around the bottleneck and base.
* **Secondary Layer:** Wrapped jars are placed inside fitted corrugated cardboard boxes with paper or foam fillers to eliminate empty spaces and prevent sliding.


* **Fragile Labeling:** All external shipping boxes must carry prominent **"Fragile - Handle with Care"** and **"This Side Up"** labels to prevent upside-down handling, which risks dislodging the internal comb pieces.

## Regulatory Considerations

As a premium product harvested from a protected ecological zone, specific domestic regulatory frameworks apply to its commercial transit.

* **Forest Department Clearance:** Large bulk shipments originating from the Khulna division require validation against official Forest Department transit permits (*pash*), which prove the honey was legally harvested by registered Moualis during the approved seasonal window.
* **BSTI Standards:** Commercial lots conform to the basic pure honey specifications set by the Bangladesh Standards and Testing Institution (BSTI).
* **Weight Class Transit Limits:** Standard single dispatches are capped across three consumer-facing weights (250g, 500g, 1kg). Consignments exceeding 20kg require specialized freight booking due to the high deadweight of glass packaging.

## International Shipping

Exporting raw, unpasteurized honey containing raw animal product elements (honeycomb, propolis) requires strict compliance with international biosecurity laws.

| Target Region | Key Requirements & Restrictions | Feasibility Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Domestic (Bangladesh)** | Standard delivery couriers; no special permits required for buyers. | Fully Supported |
| **Middle East (GCC)** | Requires Phytosanitary Certificate and standard invoice verification. | Case-by-Case Basis |
| **European Union (EU)** | Strict testing required for antibiotic residues and sugar adulteration. | Restricted / Custom Quote |
| **USA & Canada (FDA)** | Requires prior notice filing with the FDA; raw animal by-products face biosecurity checks. | Restricted / Custom Quote |

* **Customs and Biosecurity Inspection:** Many countries categorize raw honey with active comb pieces as a high-risk biological or agricultural import. Customs officials may quarantine or confiscate shipments that lack approved veterinary or phytosanitary clearance certificates.
* **Aviation Pressure Changes:** Air freight introduces significant pressure drops in cargo holds. This can cause unpasteurized honey jars to leak slightly through the seals. For international air freight, special pressure-tested lids or high-grade HDPE plastic variants may replace standard glass packaging to prevent breakage.
