# Usage
## Best uses

<section>

### Eat directly from the comb

The most traditional way to enjoy Sundarbans Raw Comb Honey is to cut a small piece of honeycomb and eat it directly. The honeycomb cells release honey naturally when chewed.

* Enjoy as a natural snack
* Consume honey and comb together
* Chew the wax and swallow or discard according to personal preference

### As a premium breakfast topping

Raw comb honey pairs well with:

* Toast and bread
* Paratha or ruti
* Pancakes and waffles
* Oatmeal and porridge
* Yogurt bowls
* Fresh fruits

### In beverages

The honey portion can be mixed into:

* Warm water
* Herbal tea
* Black tea
* Lemon water
* Ginger drinks

For best flavor and preservation of natural properties, add honey after the beverage has cooled to a warm, drinkable temperature.

### As a gourmet serving item

Comb honey is often served on:

* Cheese boards
* Dessert platters
* Fruit trays
* Special occasion breakfast spreads

Its natural honeycomb structure makes it visually appealing as well as edible.

</section>

## What to avoid

<section>

### Avoid very high heat

Excessive heating can alter the flavor, aroma, and natural characteristics of raw honey.

Avoid:

* Boiling honey
* Cooking at prolonged high temperatures
* Adding directly to extremely hot liquids

### Do not feed to infants under 12 months

Honey should not be given to babies younger than one year of age.

### Avoid contamination

To maintain quality:

* Use clean, dry utensils
* Keep the container tightly sealed
* Prevent water from entering the container

### Do not refrigerate unnecessarily

Refrigeration may accelerate crystallization and make the honey more difficult to use. Store at normal room temperature in a cool, dry place.

### Do not mistake crystallization for spoilage

Natural crystallization is common in pure honey and does not indicate that the product has gone bad. If desired, place the container in mildly warm water to soften the honey.

### Be aware of bee-product sensitivities

Individuals with known allergies or sensitivities to bee products, pollen, or related substances should exercise caution before consuming comb honey.

</section>

> **Storage recommendation:** Store in a clean, airtight container away from direct sunlight, excessive heat, moisture, and strong odors. Properly stored honey can maintain its quality for a long period.
