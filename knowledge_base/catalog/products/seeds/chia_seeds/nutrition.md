## Key Nutrients / প্রধান পুষ্টিকর উপাদানসমূহ

* **Omega-3 Fatty Acids (Alpha-Linolenic Acid - ALA):** One of the richest plant-based sources of heart-healthy Omega-3 fats, which support cardiovascular health and reduce inflammation.
* **Dietary Fiber:** Exceptionally high in soluble fiber. When soaked, chia seeds absorb up to 10–12 times their weight in water, forming a mucilaginous gel that aids digestion, promotes satiety, and assists in weight management.
* **Plant-Based Protein:** Contains a complete protein profile with all nine essential amino acids, making it an excellent nutritional supplement for vegetarians and fitness enthusiasts.
* **Essential Minerals:** Rich in Calcium, Magnesium, and Phosphorus, which are critical for maintaining bone density and metabolic function.
* **Low Glycemic Index (GI):** Due to the high fiber and healthy fat content, chia seeds have a very low glycemic impact, helping to stabilize blood sugar levels (highly beneficial for individuals managing diabetes).
