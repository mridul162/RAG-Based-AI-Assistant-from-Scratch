# Pricing
## Price Drivers / মূল্য নির্ধারণের নিয়ামকসমূহ

Several critical factors influence the retail pricing of premium chia seeds in the Bangladeshi market:

* **Import and Customs Logistics:** Because pure chia seeds are primarily imported into Bangladesh from major producing nations (such as Mexico, Brazil, Paraguay, or Australia), international freight rates, import duties, and currency exchange fluctuations significantly drive local market pricing.
* **Purity and Cleaning Grade:** Premium-grade chia seeds undergo rigorous mechanical sorting and multi-stage air-cleaning processes. This removes stems, dirt, hollow seeds, and foreign botanical materials, ensuring a purity level greater than 99%. Cheaper alternatives often skip these steps, containing notable sediment.
* **Moisture Levels and Weight Integrity:** High-quality seeds are dried precisely to a low moisture threshold (typically under 8%). This specialized processing preserves weight accuracy and prevents internal heat buildup or mold growth during long-term storage, keeping the essential oils stable.

## Value Justification / মূল্যের যৌক্তিকতা

While lower-priced seeds are often sold loose or in unsealed packets across local markets, our premium variants justify their cost through stringent quality controls:

* **Guaranteed Purity:** Zero dirt, sand, or debris. The seeds require no additional washing or manual picking before consumption.
* **Airtight, Resealable Packaging:** Packaged in heavy-duty, protective containers that shield the delicate, light-sensitive Omega-3 fats from air exposure and premature rancidity.
* **Consistent High Gel-Yield:** Properly dried and matured seeds ensure maximum hydration capacity, efficiently expanding to 10–12 times their dry weight when soaked.

## Cost vs Alternatives / অন্যান্য পুষ্টিকর উপাদানের সাথে খরচ তুলনা

When analyzed by individual nutritional yields rather than raw weight, premium chia seeds present a highly competitive cost profile against popular grocery alternatives:

* **vs. Basil Seeds (Sabja / সাবজা):** Sabja seeds generally carry a lower market price per kilogram and form a gel much faster. However, chia seeds deliver significantly higher quantities of essential plant-based Omega-3 fatty acids (ALA) and total protein per serving, offering greater dense nutritional value.
* **vs. Marine Omega-3 Supplements:** Obtaining healthy fats from clean, plant-based chia seeds provides dietary fiber, antioxidants, and trace minerals alongside the fats. This makes it a highly affordable, multi-nutrient food choice compared to isolated synthetic softgels or costly imported fish oils.
