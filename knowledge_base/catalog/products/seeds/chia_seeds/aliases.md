# Aliases
## Common Names / সাধারণ নামসমূহ

* **Chia Seeds** — Most widely used commercial and retail name in English.
* **Chia Seed** — Singular variation commonly used in product descriptions.
* **চিয়া বীজ (Chia Bij)** — Standard Bengali translation and the primary local name in Bangladesh.
* **চিয়া সীড** — Phonetic transliteration of "Chia Seed" into the Bengali script.

---

## Regional Names / আঞ্চলিক ও ভাষাগত নামসমূহ

* **Chia** — Shortened casual name used universally across English, Bengali, and Banglish text.
* **Chia Bij / Chia Beaj** — Banglish (Bengali written in the Roman alphabet) variations frequently used by local customers in digital chats and search bars.
* **Sabja Seeds / সাবজা বীজ (Misnomer)** — Though biologically a completely different product (Basil seeds), this term is widely used colloquially in Bangladesh to refer to chia seeds due to their similar water-absorbing, gel-forming behavior.

---

## Scientific & Trade Names / বৈজ্ঞানিক ও বাণিজ্যিক নামসমূহ

* *Salvia hispanica* — The official botanical/scientific name for the flowering plant in the mint family (*Lamiaceae*) native to central and southern Mexico and Guatemala.
* **Superfood Chia** — Common commercial trade and marketing name highlighting its dense nutritional profile (Omega-3, fiber, and protein).
