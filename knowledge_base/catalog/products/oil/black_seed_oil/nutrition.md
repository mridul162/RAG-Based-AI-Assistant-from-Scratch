# Nutrition
## Nutritional profile (per 100g)

The values provided below represent the typical nutritional profile of 100% pure cold-pressed black seed oil (*Nigella sativa*). As an unrefined fat extraction, it contains zero carbohydrates, proteins, or sugars.

> **Note on Variation:** Because this is a minimally processed, cold-pressed agricultural product, slight variations in fatty acid profiles can occur between manufacturing batches. These shifts are influenced by the microclimate, soil quality, and growing conditions of the specific harvest regions in Bangladesh.

## Key nutrients

Unlike standard culinary fats, the primary value of pure black seed oil lies in its potent bioactive compounds and essential fatty acid composition:

* **Thymoquinone (TQ):** The primary active phytochemical component responsible for the oil's distinct medicinal profile, antioxidant power, and anti-inflammatory attributes.
* **Linoleic Acid (Omega-6):** An essential polyunsaturated fatty acid making up over 55% of the total fat composition, critical for cellular repair and skin structural integrity.
* **Oleic Acid (Omega-9):** A monounsaturated fat that contributes to cardiovascular health and maintains the stability of the liquid oil profile.
* **Trace Phytosterols:** Plant sterols that help support healthy blood lipid levels and digestive function.
