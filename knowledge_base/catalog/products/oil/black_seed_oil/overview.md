# Overview

Black Seed Oil (Kalojira Tel / কালোজিরার তেল) is a natural oil extracted from the seeds of *Nigella sativa*, commonly known as black seed, black cumin, or kalojira. It has been used for centuries in traditional wellness practices across South Asia, the Middle East, and North Africa. This oil is typically produced through a cold-pressing process that helps preserve its naturally occurring bioactive compounds, essential fatty acids, and characteristic aroma.

Pure cold-pressed Black Seed Oil is valued for its rich nutritional profile and traditional medicinal significance. It is commonly used as a dietary oil, massage oil, hair oil, and skincare ingredient. The oil contains naturally occurring compounds such as thymoquinone, antioxidants, and unsaturated fatty acids that contribute to its widespread use in traditional health and personal care applications.

## Origin & Background

*Nigella sativa* has a long history of cultivation and use in traditional medicine systems including Ayurveda, Unani, and various folk healing traditions. In Bangladesh, kalojira is a familiar household ingredient used both as a spice and as a source of medicinal oil.

Black Seed Oil is produced by pressing carefully cleaned and dried kalojira seeds. Cold pressing is preferred because it minimizes heat exposure, helping retain the seed's natural constituents and authentic flavor profile. The resulting oil is darker and more aromatic than many common edible oils, making it easily recognizable.

The oil is often referred to by several names including Black Seed Oil, Black Cumin Oil, Nigella Oil, Kalojira Oil, and Kalojira Tel.

## Why It Matters

Black Seed Oil stands out because of its unique combination of traditional heritage, concentrated plant compounds, and versatility. Unlike many culinary oils that are primarily valued for cooking, Black Seed Oil is widely appreciated for both nutritional and personal care purposes.

Traditionally, it has been used to support immune function, respiratory wellness, digestive comfort, skin health, hair care, and overall well-being. Its naturally occurring antioxidant and anti-inflammatory compounds have made it one of the most respected therapeutic oils in many cultures.

For consumers seeking a traditional, minimally processed oil with a distinctive flavor, strong aroma, and broad range of applications, pure cold-pressed Black Seed Oil remains one of the most valued specialty oils available.
