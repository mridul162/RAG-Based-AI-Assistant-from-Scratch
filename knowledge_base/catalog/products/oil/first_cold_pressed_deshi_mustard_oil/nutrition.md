## Key Nutrients

### 1. High Monounsaturated Fatty Acids (MUFA)

Mustard oil is predominantly composed of MUFA (nearly 60%), largely driven by **Erucic acid** and **Oleic acid**. MUFAs are highly stable under heat and contribute to maintaining healthy cholesterol levels when replacing saturated fats in a balanced diet.

### 2. Balanced Omega-3 and Omega-6 Ratio

It contains an ideal, heart-healthy balance of essential polyunsaturated fatty acids:

* **Alpha-Linolenic Acid (Omega-3):** ~6-10%
* **Linoleic Acid (Omega-6):** ~15-20%

### 3. Natural Vitamin E (Tocopherols)

Cold-pressing preserves the seed's natural Vitamin E, a potent antioxidant that protects cells from oxidative stress and extends the oil's shelf life naturally without artificial preservatives.

### 4. Allyl Isothiocyanate (AITC)

While not a macronutrient, AITC is the bioactive compound responsible for the oil's signature strong, pungent aroma (*ঝাঁজ*). It possesses well-documented anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.
