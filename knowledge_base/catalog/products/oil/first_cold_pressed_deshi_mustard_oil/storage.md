# Storage Guidelines: First Cold Pressed Deshi Mustard Oil

**Product**: প্রথম চাপের দেশি সরিষার তেল (First Cold Pressed Deshi Mustard Oil)

**Updated**: 2026-06-11

## Ideal conditions

- **Temperature**: Store in a cool, dark place at 15–25°C (59–77°F). Avoid direct sunlight, heat sources, and temperature fluctuations. Refrigeration is optional and extends shelf life but may cause clouding (natural and reversible upon warming).
- **Humidity**: Keep in low to moderate humidity (<60% RH) to prevent moisture ingress.
- **Light**: Always protect from light. Use opaque or dark glass containers; light exposure accelerates oxidation and flavor degradation.
- **Air**: Minimize air exposure. Oxygen causes rancidity over time.

## Container recommendations

- **Best**: Original dark glass bottle or transfer to clean, dry, airtight dark glass or high-quality stainless steel container.
- **Acceptable**: Food-grade PET plastic bottles (short-term only).
- **Avoid**: Clear glass, reactive metals (e.g., copper, iron), or containers that previously held strong-smelling substances.
- **Sealing**: Ensure tight, leak-proof closure after every use. For long-term storage, consider vacuum sealing or topping with inert gas if available.

## Shelf life

| State          | Shelf Life                  | Notes |
|----------------|-----------------------------|-------|
| Unopened       | 12–18 months from production | Best within 12 months for peak flavor and pungency. |
| Opened         | 6–9 months                  | Use within 3–6 months for optimal taste and aroma. Refrigerate after opening for extended freshness. |

Shelf life depends on storage conditions and initial quality. Pure first cold pressed deshi mustard oil is naturally more stable than refined oils due to higher antioxidant content, but its strong pungent compounds can degrade if mishandled.

## Signs of spoilage

- **Smell**: Loss of sharp, pungent mustard aroma; development of rancid, metallic, or soapy odor.
- **Taste**: Bitter, stale, or muted flavor instead of characteristic sharp, warming pungency.
- **Appearance**: Unusual darkening, cloudiness that does not clear when warmed, or visible sediment/separation beyond normal natural settling.
- **Texture**: Increased viscosity or sliminess (rare).
- **Other**: Mold, off-color (very dark brown/black), or packaging swelling/leakage.

**Discard immediately** if any spoilage signs appear. Do not use for cooking or consumption.

## Additional tips

- Always use clean, dry spoons when dispensing to avoid introducing moisture or contaminants.
- In hot/humid Bangladeshi climates, prefer smaller pack sizes (250g/500g) for frequent use and better freshness.
- For traditional Bengali use (bhorta, frying, pickles), fresher oil provides better flavor and health benefits.
- Cold pressed nature means no added preservatives — proper storage is essential for maintaining omega-3, antioxidants, and medicinal properties.

Store properly to enjoy the full authentic taste and benefits of খাঁটি দেশি সরিষার তেল.
