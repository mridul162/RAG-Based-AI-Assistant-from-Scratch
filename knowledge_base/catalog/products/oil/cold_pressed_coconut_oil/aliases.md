# Aliases
## Common Names

* **Cold Pressed Coconut Oil** — The standard commercial and technical name for the product.
* **Virgin Coconut Oil (VCO)** — Reflects the unrefined, pure nature of the oil extracted without chemical processing.
* **Pure Coconut Oil** — Used by consumers looking for unadulterated products.
* **কোল্ড প্রেসড নারিকেল তেল** — The official Bengali translation and name for the product.
* **নারিকেল তেল** — The general Bengali term for coconut oil.

## Scientific / Trade Names

* ***Cocos nucifera* Oil** — The international botanical and scientific nomenclature for oil derived from coconuts.
* **Cold Pressed Virgin Coconut Oil (Unrefined)** — The formal trade description used in supply chain manifestos and laboratory analysis reports.
