# Pricing
## Price Drivers

* **Raw Material Dependency:** The product is subject to the fluctuating market price of raw, mature coconuts sourced from the coastal regions of Bangladesh (such as Barishal, Noakhali, and Chittagong). It takes a significant volume of fresh coconuts to yield a single liter of pure oil.
* **Extraction Methodology:** Unlike mass-produced oils that use heat and chemical solvents to maximize yield, our oil uses a mechanical cold-press method. This process prioritizes quality, purity, and nutrient preservation over high-volume output, resulting in higher production costs per milliliter.
* **No Additives or Dilution:** The oil is 100% pure, unrefined, and unbleached virgin coconut oil. The absence of cheaper filler oils or synthetic chemical stabilizers means the cost reflects entirely the expense of high-grade coconut procurement and clean processing.

## Value Justification

* **Nutritional Integrity:** The mechanical extraction operates at low temperatures, ensuring that Medium-Chain Triglycerides (MCTs), Lauric Acid, and natural antioxidants remain fully intact and biologically active.
* **Zero Chemical Refining:** Commercial oils often undergo a Refining, Bleaching, and Deodorizing (RBD) process involving harsh chemicals. Our product undergoes zero chemical alterations, maintaining its natural, sweet coconut aroma and clear texture.
* **Multi-Purpose Utility:** Because of its purity, this oil serves a dual purpose. It functions as a premium, high-smoke-point edible oil for cooking and baking, as well as a safe, clean product for topical hair care, skin moisturizing, and oil pulling.
