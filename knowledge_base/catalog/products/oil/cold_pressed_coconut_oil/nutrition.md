## Key Nutrients

* **Medium-Chain Triglycerides (MCTs):** Approximately 60% of the fatty acid content consists of MCTs, primarily **Lauric Acid** (around 48-50%), followed by Caprylic and Capric acids. Unlike long-chain fats, MCTs are rapidly absorbed by the liver and converted into immediate energy.
* **Lauric Acid:** Known for its inherent antimicrobial, antibacterial, and antifungal properties, which support immune health and topical skin barrier protection.
* **Phytochemicals and Antioxidants:** The cold-press extraction method avoids heat application, successfully retaining natural polyphenols and vitamin E (tocopherols) that protect the oil from rapid oxidation.
