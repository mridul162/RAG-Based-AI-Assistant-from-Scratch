### 1. What products do you sell?

We sell a range of natural and traditional food products across the following categories: Honey, Ghee, Oil, Spices, Jaggery, Seeds, Nuts, and Dates. Yes. Just ask us for the full product list or catalog, and we'll share it with you, organized by category. If you're interested in a specific category (e.g., "show me your honey list" or "what spices do you have?"), let us know and we can send just that section.

<section name="pricing">

### 12. How can I find out the price of a specific product?

Just tell us the product name (e.g., "What's the price of Sundarbans Kholisha Honey?" or "মধুর দাম কত?") and we'll provide the current retail price, along with the wholesale price if you're buying in bulk. Note that prices are listed per kg or per standard unit in BDT.

### 13. Do you have a price list for a whole category, like all your honey or all your nuts?

Yes — you can ask for the price list of any single category (e.g., "Honey price list" or "বাদামের দাম কত?") and we'll share retail (and wholesale, where applicable) prices for every item in that category.

### 14. Do prices change over time?

Yes. Prices may change depending on the season, market conditions, and product availability, since most of our items (especially honey, oil, jaggery, and spices) are agricultural or seasonal products. We recommend confirming the current price before placing an order, especially for larger quantities.

### 15. What's the difference between retail and wholesale pricing?

Retail prices apply to regular, smaller-quantity purchases (e.g., for personal or household use). Wholesale and dealer prices are lower per-unit rates offered for bulk purchases. To get a wholesale quote, please tell us the product and the approximate quantity you're looking to buy.

### 16. How do I get a wholesale or dealer price quote?

Send us the product name(s) and the quantity you're interested in (e.g., "I want 50 kg of Mustard Oil — what's the wholesale price?"), and we'll respond with the applicable wholesale or dealer rate for that quantity.

### 17. I don't see a product I'm looking for — do you have anything else outside this list?

The catalog above reflects our current product range across all eight categories (Honey, Ghee, Oil, Spices, Jaggery, Seeds, Nuts, Dates). If you're looking for something specific that isn't listed, feel free to ask us directly — we can let you know if it's something we carry or can source.
