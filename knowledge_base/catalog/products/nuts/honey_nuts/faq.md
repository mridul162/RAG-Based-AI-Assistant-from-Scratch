# FAQ – Honey Nuts (মধুময় বাদাম)

## 1. What are Honey Nuts (মধুময় বাদাম)?

Honey Nuts is a premium snack made from a blend of nuts, seeds, dried fruits, and natural honey. Typical ingredients may include almonds, cashews, pistachios, macadamia nuts, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, raisins, dried berries, and honey. The product combines crunch, natural sweetness, and nutritional value in a ready-to-eat format.

## 2. Is real honey used in this product?

The product is intended to be prepared using natural honey. However, the exact honey source and composition depend on the producer and batch. Customers who require detailed sourcing information should request ingredient and sourcing details directly from the seller.

## 3. How can I identify authentic Honey Nuts?

Authentic Honey Nuts generally:

* Have a mild natural honey aroma.
* Retain the visible appearance of individual nuts and seeds.
* Are crunchy rather than excessively sticky.
* Do not have an overly thick sugar coating.
* Display a balanced sweet and nutty flavor.

A transparent ingredient list and proper packaging are also positive indicators.

## 5. Is Honey Nuts a healthy snack?

Honey Nuts can be a nutrient-dense snack because nuts and seeds naturally provide healthy fats, plant-based protein, fiber, vitamins, and minerals. However, it should be consumed as part of a balanced diet and not viewed as a substitute for complete meals.

## 6. Can Honey Nuts cure diseases or provide medical benefits?

No. Honey Nuts is a food product, not a medicine. While the ingredients contain nutrients that support general health, the product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

## 7. Is Honey Nuts suitable for children?

Many children enjoy Honey Nuts because of its sweet and crunchy taste. However:

* Young children should always be supervised while eating nuts due to choking risks.
* Children with nut allergies should not consume the product.
* Portion sizes should be appropriate for the child's age.

---

## 8. Is Honey Nuts suitable for people with diabetes?

Honey Nuts contains honey, raisins, and dried fruits, all of which contribute natural sugars. Individuals with diabetes or those monitoring blood sugar levels should consult their healthcare professional regarding appropriate portion sizes and dietary suitability.

---

## 9. Does Honey Nuts contain allergens?

Yes. Honey Nuts typically contains:

* Tree nuts
* Seeds
* Honey

People with allergies to nuts or seeds should avoid consuming the product unless ingredient compatibility has been confirmed.

## 10. How should Honey Nuts be stored?

For best quality:

* Store in a cool, dry place.
* Keep the container tightly sealed after opening.
* Avoid direct sunlight, moisture, and excessive heat.
* Use clean, dry utensils when handling the product.

Proper storage helps maintain freshness and crunchiness.

## 12. Why do some pieces stick together?

Honey is naturally sticky. Mild clumping can occur, especially in warm or humid conditions. This does not necessarily indicate spoilage. However, excessive moisture, unusual odor, or visible mold may indicate that the product should not be consumed.
