## Key Nutrients (প্রধান পুষ্টি উপাদানসমূহ)

Unlike highly refined white sugar, sugarcane powder gur is unrefined and retains its natural molasses content, making it a rich source of essential minerals.

* **Iron (লোহা):** Contains approximately 11 mg per 100g, aiding in hemoglobin production.
* **Magnesium (ম্যাগনেসিয়াম):** Approximately 70–90 mg, which helps support nervous system function.
* **Potassium (পটাশিয়াম):** High concentration (up to 1050 mg), beneficial for blood pressure management and fluid balance.
* **Calcium (ক্যালসিয়াম):** Around 40–80 mg, supporting bone health.
* **Antioxidants:** Rich in selenium and other trace minerals that combat oxidative stress.

> **Note on Glycemic Index (GI):** Sugarcane Gur has a medium Glycemic Index (approximately 84), which is slightly lower than refined white sugar. However, because it is primarily composed of carbohydrates, it should still be consumed in moderation by individuals managing blood sugar levels.

## Batch and Seasonal Variations (ব্যাচ এবং মৌসুমী তারতম্য)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> As a traditional, seasonal, and minimally processed agricultural product sourced from Rajshahi, Faridpur, and Kushtia, the exact nutritional values may vary by batch.

* **Moisture & Texture:** The moisture content (3%–7%) varies depending on the winter harvesting timeline and humidity during the grinding process.
* **Color & Flavor:** The shade of brown and the intensity of the caramel-molasses flavor shift depending on the specific variety of sugarcane harvested and the localized soil conditions of the origin region.
