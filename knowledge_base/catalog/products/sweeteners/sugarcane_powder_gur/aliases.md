# Aliases
## Common Names (সাধারণ নামসমূহ)

These are the primary, widely accepted names for the product across both English and Bengali languages, commonly used in commercial labeling and standard product descriptions:

* **Sugarcane Powder Gur:** Official English product name.
* **Akher Powder Gur:** English transliteration of the Bengali name.
* **Powder Jaggery:** Generic culinary name used globally for solid jaggery that has been ground down.
* **আখের পাউডার গুড়:** Official Bengali product name.
* **পাউডার গুড়:** Frequently used shorthand in Bengali retail.
* **গুড় পাউডার:** Direct linguistic inversion used by consumers interchangeably.

## Regional Names (আঞ্চলিক নামসমূহ)

These terms represent regional variations, traditional descriptors, and informal colloquialisms used across different districts of Bangladesh (such as Rajshahi, Faridpur, and Kushtia) and neighboring communities:

* **Akher Gur (আখের গুড় / আখ গুড়):** The foundational regional term for any sugarcane-based jaggery, distinguishing it from *Khejur-er Gur* (Date Palm Jaggery).
* **Desi Gur / Deshi Gur (দেশি গুড়):** Used by locals to emphasize its nature as an unrefined, indigenous cottage-industry food product.
* **Cane Gur:** A simplified commercial or conversational term common among urban consumers.
* **Patali Gur (পাটালি গুড়):** While *Patali* technically refers to the solid block or cake form of jaggery, customers frequently use this term regionally when inquiring if the powder was milled from authentic winter-harvested cakes.

## Scientific / Trade Names (বৈজ্ঞানিক ও বাণিজ্যিক নাম)

These terms are utilized for technical inventory management, precise classification, regulatory compliance, and cross-border trade:

* **Saccharum officinarum Jaggery Powder:** The botanical/scientific classification combining the source plant name (*Saccharum officinarum*) with the processing style.
* **Non-Centrifugal Cane Sugar (NCS):** The international trade and food-safety classification for sugar produced without separating the molasses from the crystals.
* **Solidified Sugarcane Juice Powder:** The technical export declaration term detailing the raw ingredient and its state.
* **Unrefined Sugarcane Sugar:** The global market commodity label for healthy sugar alternatives.
