# Usage
## Best Uses | ব্যবহারের সেরা উপায়

Sugarcane Powder Gur is a versatile natural sweetener that can be used anywhere refined sugar or traditional solid gur is normally used. Its fine powder texture makes it especially convenient because it dissolves quickly and mixes evenly.

### Beverages

* Tea (black tea, milk tea, herbal tea)
* Coffee
* Warm milk
* Hot chocolate
* Smoothies and milkshakes
* Lemon water and traditional drinks

### Traditional Bangladeshi Foods

* Payesh (rice pudding)
* Pitha varieties
* Semai
* Firni
* Chira (flattened rice) preparations
* Homemade sweets and desserts

### Baking & Cooking

* Cakes
* Cookies
* Muffins
* Pancakes
* Bread recipes
* Dessert toppings
* Sweet sauces and syrups

### Everyday Sweetening

* Oatmeal and porridge
* Yogurt
* Fruit bowls
* Breakfast cereals
* Nut and seed mixes

## Recipe Ideas | রেসিপি আইডিয়া

### Sweet Milk with Gur

**Ingredients**

* 1 cup warm milk
* 1–2 teaspoons Sugarcane Powder Gur

**Method**

1. Warm the milk.
2. Stir in the powder gur until dissolved.
3. Serve immediately.

### Gur Tea (গুড় চা)

**Ingredients**

* 1 cup brewed tea
* 1–2 teaspoons Sugarcane Powder Gur

**Method**

1. Prepare tea as usual.
2. Add gur instead of refined sugar.
3. Stir well and serve.

### Quick Chira-Gur Breakfast

**Ingredients**

* Flattened rice (chira)
* Sugarcane Powder Gur
* Banana slices
* Milk or yogurt

**Method**

1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
2. Mix thoroughly.
3. Serve as a quick breakfast or snack.

### Gur Oatmeal

**Ingredients**

* Cooked oats
* 1 tablespoon Sugarcane Powder Gur
* Nuts and fruits

**Method**

1. Prepare oatmeal.
2. Add powder gur while warm.
3. Top with nuts and fruit.

### Traditional Payesh

Use Sugarcane Powder Gur as the sweetening ingredient in rice pudding for a richer caramel-like flavor than white sugar.

## What to Avoid | যা এড়িয়ে চলবেন

### Avoid Excessive Consumption

Although less processed than refined sugar, Sugarcane Powder Gur is still a concentrated sweetener and should be consumed in moderation.

### Avoid Exposure to Moisture

The powder can absorb moisture from the air, leading to:

* Clumping
* Hardening
* Reduced shelf stability

Always keep the container tightly sealed.

### Do Not Store Near Strong Odors

Gur can absorb surrounding smells during storage. Keep it away from strong-smelling foods and chemicals.

### Avoid Wet Spoons

Using wet utensils can introduce moisture and shorten storage life.

### Not a Low-Sugar Product

Individuals monitoring sugar intake, including those following medically prescribed diets, should consider the product's sugar content when planning consumption.

### Storage Recommendations

* Store in an airtight container.
* Keep in a cool, dry place.
* Protect from direct sunlight and humidity.
* Reseal the package immediately after use.

## Usage Tips | ব্যবহারিক পরামর্শ

* Add to hot beverages for quick dissolution.
* Mix into batters and doughs for even sweetness.
* Use in winter desserts and traditional recipes for authentic flavor.
* Start with slightly less than your usual sugar amount, as gur provides both sweetness and a richer flavor profile.
* Break up any natural clumps by stirring or gently crushing before use if needed.
