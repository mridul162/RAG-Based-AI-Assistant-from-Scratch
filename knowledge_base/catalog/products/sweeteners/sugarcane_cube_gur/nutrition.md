# Nutrition
## Nutritional Profile / পুষ্টিগুণ (per 100g)

Traditional sugarcane cube gur (jaggery) is an unrefined sweetener. Unlike refined white sugar, it retains its natural molasses content, which provides trace minerals and vitamins.

The nutritional values below are representative averages per 100g:

| Nutrient | Amount per 100g |
| --- | --- |
| **Energy / ক্যালরি** | ~383 kcal |
| **Carbohydrates / কার্বোহাইড্রেট** | 95g - 98g |
| *— Sucrose* | 65g - 85g |
| *— Fructose & Glucose* | 10g - 15g |
| **Protein / প্রোটিন** | 0.4g |
| **Fat / ফ্যাট** | 0.1g |
| **Water / জলীয় অংশ** | 3g - 4g |

> **Batch & Seasonal Variation Note:** Because this gur is produced using traditional artisanal methods from fresh sugarcane juice across Rajshahi, Faridpur, and Kushtia, exact nutritional values (especially moisture content and sugar ratios) will vary slightly by batch, seasonal harvest time, and soil conditions.

## Key Nutrients / প্রধান পুষ্টি উপাদানসমূহ

Because sugarcane juice is concentrated without rigorous chemical refining, the following micro-nutrients are preserved in the final cubes:

* **Iron (Fe):** Essential for hemoglobin production. Regular consumption helps meet daily iron requirements.
* **Magnesium (Mg):** Aids in nervous system function and muscle relaxation.
* **Potassium (K):** Helps manage fluid balance and supports healthy blood pressure levels.
* **Calcium (Ca) & Phosphorus (P):** Present in trace amounts to support bone health.
* **Antioxidants:** The dark brown caramel-molasses color indicates the presence of plant polyphenols, which help combat oxidative stress.

| Feature / Nutrient | Sugarcane Cube Gur | Refined White Sugar | Honey (Natural) |

| **Mineral Content** | High (Iron, Potassium, Magnesium) | None (Empty calories) | Trace minerals & vitamins |
| **Glycemic Index (GI)** | **~84 (High)** | **~65 (Medium-High)** | **~58 (Medium)** |

| **Primary Use** | Pitha, Payesh, Tea, Traditional Bengali sweets | General baking, beverages | Teas, spreads, medicinal uses |

> **Health Disclaimer for Customers:** While Sugarcane Cube Gur is significantly healthier than refined white sugar due to its mineral and antioxidant content, it remains a high-glycemic sweetener composed mostly of sucrose. It should be consumed in moderation, and individuals with diabetes should consult a healthcare professional before including it in their diet.
