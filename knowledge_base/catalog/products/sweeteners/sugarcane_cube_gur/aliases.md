# Aliases
## Common Names / সাধারণ নামসমূহ

These are the standard names used across Bangladesh and international markets to identify unrefined sugarcane jaggery in cube form:

* **Sugarcane Cube Gur:** Standard international and e-commerce name.
* **Akher Cube Gur (আখের কিউব গুড়):** The primary commercial and colloquial name combining the Bengali term for sugarcane (*akh*) and the English structural description (*cube*).
* **Cube Gur (কিউব গুড়):** Shortened term focusing on the modern, portion-controlled cube shape.
* **Akher Gur / Akher Gurr (আখের গুড় / আখ গুড়):** The broader name for all types of sugarcane jaggery, frequently applied by customers who do not specify the block format.

---

## Regional Names / আঞ্চলিক নামসমূহ

Jaggery nomenclature varies significantly based on regional production styles, dialects, and shapes across Bangladesh and the wider subcontinent:

* **Patali Gur (পাটালি গুড়):** Widely used in Rajshahi, Faridpur, and Kushtia. While traditionally referring to solid, flat blocks or broken discs of jaggery, customers commonly use this term interchangeably when searching for molded cube jaggery.
* **Deshi Gur / Desi Gur (দেশি গুড়):** Used nationally to distinguish local, unrefined Bangladeshi sugarcane jaggery from highly processed commercial sugars or imported alternatives.
* **Cane Jaggery / Cane Gur:** Common terms used by non-resident Bangladeshis, expats, and international consumers across English-speaking regions.

---

## Scientific & Trade Names / বৈজ্ঞানিক ও বাণিজ্যিক নাম

Technical descriptions used for customs documentation, trade logs, and regulatory compliance:

* **Unrefined Sugarcane Carbohydrate Concentrate:** Industry and food science description for raw sugarcane juice boiled down without centrifugation or chemical bleaching.
* **Non-Centrifugal Sugar (NCS):** International trade classification used to categorize traditional sugars that retain natural molasses.
* **Saccharum officinarum Jaggery:** Trade name incorporating the scientific botanical name of the source plant, *Saccharum officinarum* (Sugarcane).
