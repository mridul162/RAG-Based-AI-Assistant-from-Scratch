# Pricing
## Price Drivers / মূল্য নির্ধারণের নিয়ামকসমূহ

Several critical factors drive the cost of our Premium Sugarcane Cube Gur, reflecting its traditional production methods and raw material sourcing:

* **Seasonal Sugarcane Pricing:** Because production is directly linked to the seasonal sugarcane harvest in Bangladesh, raw crop procurement costs fluctuate based on annual yields, weather conditions, and regional demand.
* **Artisanal Craftsmanship & Processing:** Unlike industrially mass-produced refined white sugar, this gur is made by skilled local artisans who slowly boil and reduce fresh sugarcane juice. Molding the thick mixture manually into uniform cube shapes adds significant labor costs compared to bulk blocks (patali).
* **Regional Sourcing:** The raw sugarcane is exclusively sourced from Bangladesh's premium sugarcane-producing belt—specifically Rajshahi, Faridpur, and Kushtia. Transporting and selecting clean, high-sucrose stalks from these premium regions drives up early-stage procurement costs.
* **Zero Adulteration Assurance:** No industrial chemical bleaching agents, artificial colorants, or cheap sugar syrup fillers are introduced to increase volume. Maintaining this strict unrefined purity inherently reduces final production yield per metric ton of raw cane, raising the per-kilogram price.

## Value Justification / মূল্যের যৌক্তিকতা

At **300 BDT per 1kg variant**, this product offers a clear value proposition for health-conscious consumers and traditional cooks:

* **Portion Control Convenience:** The pre-molded cube format eliminates the messy, physically intensive task of scraping or chopping traditional large jaggery blocks. One cube can be directly dropped into tea, coffee, or individual dessert portions.
* **Longer Integrity Preservation:** Regular block gur exposed to air degrades quickly across broken surfaces. The individual cube structure reduces overall air exposure per piece, helping the batch maintain its natural aroma, texture, and caramel notes for longer periods under correct storage conditions.
* **Traceability:** Every single cube belongs to a batch safely routed back to prime agricultural regions (Rajshahi, Faridpur, Kushtia), ensuring authentic Bangladeshi taste profiles rather than generic, heavily processed alternatives.
