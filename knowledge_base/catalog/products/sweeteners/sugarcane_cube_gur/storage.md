# Storage
## Ideal Conditions / আদর্শ পরিবেশ

Sugarcane Cube Gur is highly hygroscopic, meaning it readily absorbs moisture from the air. High humidity and heat will cause the cubes to soften, liquefy, or develop mold.

* **Temperature / তাপমাত্রা:** Store in a cool, dry place between **15°C and 25°C**. Avoid placing it near heat sources like stoves, ovens, or direct sunlight.
* **Humidity / আর্দ্রতা:** Keep away from high humidity. This is especially critical during the Bangladeshi monsoon season when atmospheric moisture is high.
* **Refrigeration / রেফ্রিজারেশন:** Refrigeration is highly recommended for long-term storage or during humid weather to keep the cubes firm and prevent melting.

---

## Container Recommendations / পাত্রের ব্যবহার

Proper containment ensures the cubes do not absorb ambient moisture or surrounding food odors.

* **Best Options:** Use clean, completely dry, airtight glass jars or food-grade airtight plastic containers. Glass is preferred as it does not affect the natural aroma of the gur.
* **Handling:** Always use a dry spoon or clean, dry hands to take out the cubes. Introducing even a single drop of water into the container can trigger fermentation and mold growth.
* **Packaging Note:** If keeping the product in its original packaging, ensure the zip-lock or seal is entirely pressed shut after every single use.

---

## Shelf Life / সংরক্ষণকাল

The shelf life of traditional sugarcane gur depends heavily on how well it is protected from air and moisture.

| Storage Method | Unopened Shelf Life | Opened Shelf Life |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Pantry (Cool, Dry Place)** | 6 Months | 2 to 3 Months |
| **Refrigerator (Chilled)** | 12 Months | 6 Months |

---

## Signs of Spoilage / নষ্ট হওয়ার লক্ষণসমূহ

Customers should check for the following indicators before using old or poorly stored gur:

* **Mold Growth / ছত্রাক:** White, green, or grey fuzzy patches on the surface of the cubes. If mold is present, the entire batch must be discarded.
* **Sour Smell or Taste / টক গন্ধ বা স্বাদ:** Pure sugarcane gur should smell like rich caramel and molasses. A sour, alcohol-like, or fermented odor indicates that moisture has triggered yeast fermentation.
* **Excessive Liquefaction / গলে যাওয়া:** While minor surface stickiness is normal in hot weather, if the cubes completely melt into a watery, runny liquid at the bottom of the jar, the texture has degraded significantly.
* **White Powdery Crust / সাদা আস্তরণ:** A dry, salty, or overly white crust on the outside usually indicates moisture loss or the crystallization of non-sugar elements. While not always harmful, it degrades flavor and texture.
