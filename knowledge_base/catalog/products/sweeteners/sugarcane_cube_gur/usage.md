# Usage

## Best Uses (সেরা ব্যবহার)

Sugarcane Cube Gur (আখের কিউব গুড়) is a versatile traditional sweetener that can be used anywhere refined sugar is normally used, while also adding a distinctive caramel-like flavor and aroma.

### Hot Beverages

* Sweeten tea instead of white sugar
* Add to milk tea for a richer taste
* Mix into warm milk during winter
* Use in traditional herbal drinks

### Traditional Bengali Foods

* Pitha (ভাপা পিঠা, পাটিসাপটা, চিতই পিঠা)
* Payesh (rice pudding)
* Semai and firni
* Halwa preparations
* Homemade sweets and desserts

### Everyday Cooking

* Sweeten porridge or oatmeal
* Use in baking recipes
* Add to yogurt or curd
* Mix into sauces, marinades, or chutneys that benefit from a deep sweetness

### Natural Sweetener Alternative

* Replace refined sugar in beverages
* Use in homemade energy snacks
* Sweeten fruit preparations and desserts

### How to Use

1. Break or grate the cube if needed.
2. Add directly to hot liquids for faster dissolution.
3. For desserts and cooking, dissolve in a small amount of warm water if a smooth consistency is desired.
4. Taste and adjust before adding more.

## Recipe Ideas (রেসিপি আইডিয়া)

### Traditional Gur Tea

**Ingredients**

* 1 cup hot tea
* 5–10 g Sugarcane Cube Gur

**Method**

1. Prepare tea as usual.
2. Add gur while the tea is hot.
3. Stir until dissolved and serve.

### Gur Payesh

**Ingredients**

* Rice
* Milk
* Sugarcane Cube Gur

**Method**

1. Cook rice in milk until soft.
2. Remove from high heat.
3. Add grated gur gradually and stir well.
4. Simmer briefly and serve.

### Winter Pitha Filling

**Ingredients**

* Grated coconut
* Crushed gur

**Method**

1. Heat coconut and gur together.
2. Stir until combined.
3. Use as filling for traditional pitha.

### Gur Yogurt Bowl

**Ingredients**

* Plain yogurt
* Crushed gur
* Fruits or nuts (optional)

**Method**

1. Add crushed gur to yogurt.
2. Mix lightly.
3. Top with fruits or nuts and serve.

## What to Avoid (যা এড়িয়ে চলবেন)

### Excessive Consumption

Although less processed than refined sugar, Sugarcane Cube Gur is still a concentrated sweetener and should be consumed in moderation as part of a balanced diet.

### Exposure to Moisture

Avoid:

* Storing in humid environments
* Leaving the package open for extended periods
* Contact with wet spoons or utensils

Moisture can cause clumping, softening, or spoilage.

### Improper Storage

For best quality:

* Store in an airtight container
* Keep in a cool, dry place
* Protect from direct sunlight and heat

### Adding to Very High-Heat Caramelization Stages

When preparing delicate desserts, add gur according to recipe instructions, as prolonged high heat may alter flavor and color.

### Assuming Identical Sweetness to Refined Sugar

Sugarcane gur provides both sweetness and flavor. Depending on the recipe, the caramel and molasses notes may change the final taste compared to white sugar, which is often desirable in traditional dishes but may not suit every preparation.
