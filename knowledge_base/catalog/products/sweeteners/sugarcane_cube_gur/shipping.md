# Shipping
## Temperature Requirements / তাপমাত্রা সংবেদনশীলতা

Sugarcane Cube Gur is highly sensitive to extreme temperatures and high relative humidity during transit.

* **Melting Risk:** Exposure to temperatures above **30°C** for extended periods will cause the unrefined cubes to sweat, soften, or lose their structural shape.
* **Seasonal Dispatch Restrictions:** During the peak summer and monsoon months (May to September), transit delays in unventilated courier vehicles can lead to partial melting or moisture absorption. Shipments are prioritized for early morning dispatches, and climate-controlled storage is maintained at hubs prior to final delivery.

---

## Packaging Notes / প্যাকেজিং নির্দেশিকা

To prevent structural damage and environmental exposure during logistics handling, the product requires distinct multi-layered protective packaging:

* **Primary Packaging:** The cubes are strictly sealed inside a moisture-barrier, food-grade zipper pouch or heat-sealed plastic lining to isolate the product from ambient atmospheric air.
* **Secondary Packaging:** To maintain the shape of the cubes and protect them from crushing forces under heavier boxes, the primary bag is placed within rigid, corrugated outer boxes.
* **Cushioning:** Adequate eco-friendly filler or paper wrapping is applied inside the box to minimize movement and friction between the packages during local and national transit.

---

## Regulatory Considerations / নিয়ন্ত্রক সতর্কতা

* **Product Classification:** Shipped under the general agricultural food product / unrefined natural sweetener classification.
* **Perishability status:** Non-perishable under stable, dry conditions, but handled as a moisture-sensitive food item.
* **Local Documentation:** Every domestic commercial consignment from the Rajshahi, Faridpur, or Kushtia manufacturing facilities travels with a standard commercial invoice, a batch-specific quality assurance certificate, and clearance tags confirming compliance with Bangladeshi food safety standards.

---

## International Shipping / আন্তর্জাতিক শিপিং

Exporting Sugarcane Cube Gur outside of Bangladesh requires strict adherence to international sanitary and phytosanitary regulations:

* **Customs Restrictions:** Many destination countries strictly regulate or restrict the import of unrefined agricultural products, artisanal sugars, or jaggery by individual consumers.
* **Mandatory Documentation:** International shipments require a comprehensive commercial invoice, a detailed country of origin certificate (Bangladesh), a phytosanitary certificate, and an accurate FDA/Food Safety declaration matching the destination country's imports protocols.
* **Prohibited Methods:** Due to unpredictable customs clearing delays and high humidity exposure at sea ports, international ocean freight is generally discouraged for small batches. Express air freight via specialized international couriers is recommended to preserve the product's dry texture and authentic aroma.
